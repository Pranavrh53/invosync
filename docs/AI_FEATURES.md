# AI Features in InvoSync

InvoSync now includes advanced AI-powered features to streamline invoicing and provide financial insights.

## 1. AI Invoice Assistant (Magic Drafter)
**Location:** Invoice Builder (New Invoice Page)

The AI Invoice Assistant allows you to generate complete invoices from simple natural language descriptions.

### How to use:
1. Go to **Create New Invoice**.
2. In the "AI Magic Drafter" box, type a description like:
   > "Website redesign for Acme Corp, 40 hours at $50/hr, plus $200 for hosting"
3. Click the **Wand Icon** or press Enter.
4. The AI will automatically:
   - Create line items with descriptions, quantities, and prices.
   - Calculate totals and taxes.
   - Populate the invoice form.

## 2. Predictive Cash Flow Forecast
**Location:** Dashboard (Advanced Analytics Section)

This widget uses historical invoice data to predict future revenue trends.

### Features:
- **Next Month Projection:** Estimates revenue for the upcoming month based on a 6-month growth trend.
- **Incoming Cash:** Calculates confirmed revenue from unpaid invoices due in the next 30 days.
- **Confidence Score:** Displays an AI confidence metric based on data consistency.
- **Trend Indicators:** Shows growth percentage trends.

## 3. Document Intelligence (Previously Added)
**Location:** Document AI Page

- Drag and drop invoices to extract data automatically.
- Confidence scoring for extracted fields.
